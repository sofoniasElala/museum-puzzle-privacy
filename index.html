Privacy Policy

Museum Puzzle

Last updated: February 12, 2025

Introduction

Museum Puzzle ("we", "our", or "the app") is a jigsaw puzzle game featuring famous paintings. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Museum Puzzle does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain information:

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version of the app. AdMob may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (such as IDFA on iOS or Advertising ID on Android)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, screen size)
  • General location data (based on IP address)
  • App usage data for ad personalization

This information is used to show relevant advertisements. You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases (RevenueCat)

We use RevenueCat to process in-app purchases. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Purchase history and transaction data
  • Device identifiers for purchase verification
  • App usage data related to purchases

This information is used solely to manage your premium subscription and restore purchases across devices.

For more information, see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

Photo Library Access

Your photos stay on your device.

The custom photo puzzle feature allows you to create puzzles from your own images. When you select a photo:

  • The image is processed entirely on your device
  • Photos are never uploaded to any server
  • We do not store, access, or share your photos
  • Photo access is only used when you explicitly choose to create a custom puzzle

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as described in this policy:

  • Google AdMob receives advertising-related data as described above
  • RevenueCat receives purchase-related data as described above

Both services operate under their own privacy policies and handle data according to their standard practices.

Data Storage

Your puzzle progress and app preferences are stored locally on your device. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.

Children's Privacy

Museum Puzzle is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The app does not require any personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
